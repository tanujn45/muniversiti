World Summits

Future leaders under development

World Summits have had their inception with the belief that mere thinking of ideas does not impart a change in the society but speaking them out and they being acted upon does. This journey of thousands of students from diverse demographics to discuss global issues is crafted so that thoughts from varied minds could converge.

Muniversiti’s conferences remain unique because we pass down the resolutions prepared by delegates in each conference to our partnering NGOs, who possess the skills and resources required to implement those solutions. We associate with people working in fields ranging from gender equality to climate change in order to ensure none of the ideas proposed by the youth goes in vain.
